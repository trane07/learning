How to Navigate

Remember "a href=" lets you navigate to:

  • Another section: "a href="#_">_Name"

    • (above link takes you to navigation links at bottom)

  • Another page: "a href="_.html">_Name"

    • (above link takes you to homepage)

  • Another website: "a href="http://www._.com">_Name"

    • (above link takes you to an important message. teehee)


Navigating to Section

Remember to reference "a href="#_">_Name".

Then, for that section, do "div id=_"

Don't forget the "/div" at the end!

This option navigates to another section on the same page.


Navigating to Page

Remember the ".html" page must exist!


Navigating to Website

You have to write "target=_" after link with these options to open it:

  • "_blank" opens in new tab
  • "_self" opens in same tab
  • "_parent" opens in parent frame
  • "_top" opens in full body of window
