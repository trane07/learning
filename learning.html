What about Media?

Adding "img"es

To add an "img," you need a "src," which is typically a link (gif/jpg).

This can be a gif or an image address, placed after "img src=".

But don't be posting anything sussy, or as my pookie bear would say...


Don't forget the "/>" right after the link!


Adding "video"s

To add a "video," you will also need a "src," or a link (mp4) to it.

You will also have to add "height=" & "width=" to get the right sizing!

Don't forget to add "controls" after "video src=" to let the user pause/play/stop the video.
